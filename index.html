Priyanka Golia

Ph.D. Candidate

Hi ! I am a Joint Ph.D candidate with Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur,India, and National University of Singapore. My research advisers are Subhajit Roy and Kuldeep S. Meel.

My research interest lies in the area of Boolean Function Synthesis, Constraint Solving and Sampling, and Knowledge Compilation.
